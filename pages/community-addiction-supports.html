Community Addiction Supports

Community-based addiction supports, harm reduction services, outreach, rehabilitation supports, housing-related addiction supports, and local HSE addiction services.

Community Support Services

Merchants Quay Ireland

Provides addiction services, harm reduction programmes, homelessness support, treatment options, and community outreach throughout Ireland.

Phone: 01 524 0160 Visit Website

Tiglin

Supports people affected by addiction, homelessness, and other life challenges through rehabilitation, housing, education, employment, outreach, youth services, and family support.

Phone: 0404 40010 Visit Website

Ana Liffey Drug Project

Low-threshold harm reduction and addiction support service for people affected by drug and alcohol use.

Phone: 01 878 6899 Freephone: 1800 78 68 28 office@aldp.ie Visit Website

DrugWise Harm Reduction Information

Information explaining harm reduction approaches, including safer drug use, needle exchange, drug checking, reducing overdose risk, and reducing drug-related harm.

This is an information resource rather than a direct Irish support service.

Harm reduction information Visit Website

Chrysalis Community Drug Project

Dublin-based community drug project offering harm reduction, therapeutic supports, and help for people affected by addiction and related difficulties.

Local HSE Addiction Services

HSE and community addiction services vary by area. The HSE Drugs and Alcohol Helpline can help people find support and treatment options near them.
