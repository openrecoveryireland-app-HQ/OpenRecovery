Not sure where to start?

Choose the option that sounds closest. You do not need to know the name of a service.

I need to talk to someone today Helplines, information services, and places that can point you toward support. I want to stop drinking or using Addiction supports, meetings, treatment options, helplines, and community services. I think I need treatment Residential treatment centres and structured addiction treatment services. I need a meeting Peer support meetings such as AA, NA, LifeRing, SMART Recovery, Al-Anon, and Gamblers Anonymous. I need counselling or professional help Counsellor directories, professional support, helplines, and service finders. I need community addiction support Harm reduction, outreach, local HSE addiction services, rehabilitation supports, and community projects. I feel unsafe or in crisis Mental health crisis supports, emotional support services, and urgent support contacts. I am worried about abuse or safety Domestic abuse, sexual violence, child protection, coercive control, and safety supports. I need housing, money, welfare, or legal help Housing advice, homelessness support, money advice, welfare information, legal advice, and advocacy.
