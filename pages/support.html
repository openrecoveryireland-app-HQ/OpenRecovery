General Contacts & Official Sources

This section lists official organisations and public information sources used on the alcohol statistics page.

These contacts are included so people can check the original source, look for more information, or contact the organisation directly if they want to explore the data further.

Central Statistics Office

Central Statistics Office

The Central Statistics Office publishes official Irish statistics. This is included here because someone reading the alcohol statistics page may want to check the source, look up further information, or explore official data for themselves.

The statistics page can link back to this section whenever CSO figures are used.

Health Service Executive

Health Service Executive

The Health Service Executive provides official Irish health information, including alcohol guidance, low-risk drinking guidelines, self-assessment tools, and advice on getting support.

This is included here because alcohol statistics on the Mosaic site may refer to HSE guidance, and readers may want to check the original HSE information for themselves.

HSE Live: 1800 700 700

Healthy Ireland

Healthy Ireland

Healthy Ireland is a Government of Ireland health and wellbeing programme. It publishes survey information on health behaviours in Ireland, including alcohol use and alcohol-related harm.

This is included here because the alcohol statistics page may refer to Healthy Ireland survey figures, and readers may want to check the original source or explore the wider survey for themselves.

Source: Healthy Ireland Survey 2025 Visit Website

Health Research Board

Health Research Board

The Health Research Board is Ireland's lead agency for health research and health information. It publishes official data and reports on alcohol and drug treatment through the National Drug Treatment Reporting System.

This is included here because the alcohol statistics page uses HRB alcohol treatment figures, and readers may want to check the original source or explore the wider HRB data for themselves.

Phone: 085 219 7917

Health Information and Quality Authority

Health Information and Quality Authority

HIQA is an independent authority that works to improve health and social care services in Ireland. It publishes evidence-informed reports, including reports used to inform Ireland's low-risk alcohol guidelines.

This is included here because the alcohol statistics page uses HIQA figures on alcohol-attributable deaths and hospital admissions in Ireland.
