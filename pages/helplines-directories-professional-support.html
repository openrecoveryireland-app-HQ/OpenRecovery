Helplines & Advice

HSE Drugs and Alcohol Helpline

Confidential information and support for anyone worried about alcohol or drug use, including people looking for help for themselves or someone else.

Phone: 1800 459 459 helpline@hse.ie Visit Website

Professional Support

Addiction Counsellors of Ireland

Professional body and directory for addiction counsellors and addiction support professionals in Ireland.

This is useful for people who want to look for a counsellor they can contact directly and, where suitable, arrange an appointment to speak with them.

Phone: 01 797 9187 info@addictioncounsellors.ie Visit Website

HSE National Drug Treatment Centre

HSE specialist addiction treatment centre in Dublin providing treatment, guidance, training, and support in the area of substance misuse.

Phone: 01 648 8600 nationaldrugtreatmentcentre@healthmail.ie Visit Website
