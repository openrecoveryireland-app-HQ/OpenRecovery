Talk to Someone Now

Samaritans

Free, confidential emotional support available day or night for anyone experiencing distress, loneliness, anxiety, or struggling to cope.

Phone: 116 123 jo@samaritans.ie Visit Website

Pieta

Support for people experiencing suicidal thoughts, self-harm, suicide bereavement, or severe emotional distress.

Phone: 1800 247 247 Text: HELP to 51444 info@pieta.ie Visit Website

Text About It

Free, anonymous, 24/7 text support for anyone who needs a calming chat or immediate support by message.

A 24/7 information line for mental health supports and services. This is an information line, not a counselling service.
