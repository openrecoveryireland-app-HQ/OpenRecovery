Peer Support Meetings

Peer-led recovery meetings and support groups for people affected by alcohol, drugs, gambling, or someone else’s addiction.

Meeting Supports

Alcoholics Anonymous

Peer-led meetings for people who want to stop drinking.

Phone: 01 842 0700 Visit Website

Narcotics Anonymous

Peer-led meetings for people seeking freedom from active addiction, regardless of the substance involved.

Phone: 01 672 8000 Visit Website

LifeRing Ireland

Secular peer-support meetings for people who want to overcome alcohol, drug, or behavioural addictions.

Peer support meetings Visit Website

SMART Recovery Ireland

Self-management recovery meetings using practical tools for addictive behaviours, including alcohol, drugs, gambling, and other problems.

Online and local meetings Visit Website

Al-Anon Family Groups

Support for families and friends affected by someone else’s drinking.

Phone: 01 873 2699 Visit Website

Gamblers Anonymous

Peer-led recovery meetings for people experiencing problems with gambling and seeking support from others with similar experiences.

Phone: 01 872 1133 Visit Website
