Domestic Abuse & Coercive Control

Women’s Aid

National support service for women affected by domestic abuse, coercive control, relationship abuse, and violence. Women’s Aid offers confidential support, information, guidance, and links to services.

National Freephone Helpline: 1800 341 900 helpline@womensaid.ie Visit Website

Men’s Aid Ireland

Confidential support and information for men experiencing domestic abuse, coercive control, relationship violence, or safety concerns.

National Confidential Support Line: 01 554 3811 hello@mensaid.ie Visit Website
